## ✨ New

**Mobile floating button.** A round play button now floats over the note on mobile. Tap it to play from your work marker (or the start) without swiping over to the sidebar player — it sits in the top-right corner and keeps the editor focused, so the on-screen keyboard stays up.

- **Two tap modes** (Settings, mobile only):
  - **Always from marker** (default) — every tap (re)starts the track from the marker.
  - **Smart play/pause** — tap plays from the marker while paused, pauses while playing.
- Enable/disable toggle. Fully localized (EN/RU).

## 🐛 Fixes

- **Track-switching race** — rapidly switching tracks could briefly show the wrong track or apply another track's marker. Loads are now guarded by a token, so a superseded load can't touch the player.
- **Waveform decode cache** — a transient decode failure no longer sticks forever; the waveform retries on the next load instead of staying blank.
- **Playhead on a paused seek** — the cursor now moves when you seek while paused (previously only the time readout updated).
- Internal cleanup: removed dead code, merged duplicate pointer branches, tidied imports.
